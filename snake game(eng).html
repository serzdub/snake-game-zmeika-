HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

  • Use W, A, S, D keys to move
  • Press Space to pause the game
  • You cannot reverse direction (from W to S or vice versa, from A to D or vice versa)

GAME RULES

  • Collect red food to grow longer
  • Each food gives +10 points
  • Every 50 points increases level and speed
  • Avoid collisions with walls and yourself
  • Game ends when you collide

DIFFICULTY LEVELS

  • Easy - Slow speed, perfect for beginners
  • Normal - Standard game speed
  • Hard - Fast speed, for experienced players
  • Extreme - Maximum speed, real challenge
